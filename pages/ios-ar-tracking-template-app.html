iOS AR Tracking Template App

We developed an iOS App using ARKit and OpenCV framework as a template for customized AR tracked visualization and interaction on everyone's portable device. We have been utilized this in template in disciplines of engineering and visual art's showcase.

Motivation

Wanting to integrate spatial visualizations and interactions into your projects? Finding online AR design resources be not reliable and want to have a more engineering-level control of your customized AR? We developed a template iOS AR app by using tracking markers to anchor your assets in your project space to speed up this process.


Development

The app leverages ARKit for holding the AR session and OpenCV for marker-based spaital pose tracking. We brigded OpenCV to swift with a thrid party resouce, OpenCV SPM Package. There are multiple control-able parameters for the tracking pipeline, including pixel buffer size, marker ids (DICT_ARUCO_MIP_36h12), and filters (one-euro, SLERP, average-window).

The raw tracking results are visualzable through a primitive RBG axis on each tracked marker, following OpenGL and ARKit's coordinate system.
