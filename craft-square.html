Meet CRAFT^2

HCI products are researched based on humans, serve humans, and offer the most immediate, tangible demonstrations. Sophisticated processes of engineering computation and fabrication lie beneath the HCI interfaces, which must also be infused with the aesthetics and artistry of design. Uniting these dimensions is CRAFT^2's principle.

CRAFT^2 is our broader academic platform within the field of HCI. Horizontally, our research and outreach span a wide range of topics, including open-source mechatronics, personal fabrication, social empowerment, education, visual design, and more. Vertically, we engage across multiple levels of professional depth and context, from academic conferences and campus activities to community outreach and institutional talks. Our members include high school students, undergraduates, graduates, and professionals all around the world.
